Kukenan Tepui

Tepui Kukenán or Cuquenán is a tepui in the Santa Elena de Uairén parish, Gran Sabana municipality, Bolívar state, in the Guayana region, part of the South American country of Venezuela.

Roraima Tepui

Mount Roraima, also known as Roraima tepuy, Roraima hill or simply Roraima, with 2810 m s. no. m., is the highest point of the chain of plateaus tepuis (tabular mountains) of the Sierra de Pacaraima, in South America.

Auyantepui

Auyan-tepuy or Auyantepuy, which in the Pemon language means the "mountain of the devil", is a tepuy in the Santa Elena de Uairén parish of the Gran Sabana Municipality of Bolívar state, within the Canaima National Park.
