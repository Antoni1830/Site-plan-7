Quebrada Pacheco

Right at kilometer 238 of the highway. Originally it was called Quebrada Arapán and it consists of two waterfalls on the edge of the road, ideal for a refreshing stop.

Salto-Kama

The waterfall is 50 meters high and is next to the road. When getting out of the car, all you can see is a gentle stream, which disappears on the horizon.

Quebrada de Jaspe

At kilometer 273. Here you have to walk a few minutes to admire a huge reddish jasper slab through which an enchanting river falls.
