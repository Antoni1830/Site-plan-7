Have the wonderful opportunity to know the "Gran Sabana" with your family"

La Gran Sabana is a region located in the southeast of Venezuela, in the Guianas massif, also to the south-east of Bolívar State, and which extends to the border with Brazil and Guyana. The Gran Sabana has an extension of 10,820 km², and is part of one of the largest national parks in Venezuela, the Canaima National Park. The average temperature is around 20 °C, but at night it can drop to 13 °C, and in some of the higher places, depending on the weather, it can drop a little more.
