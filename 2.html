Services✈️

In order to ensure the comfort of our passengers, and the enjoyment of their trip; Whether for business or pleasure, at Italviajes we offer Passenger Assistance Service at the National and International Airport of Maiquetia. In addition to the passenger assistance service, each of our agencies becomes assistance, advice and help points for our passengers.

Trips✈️

For this type of transport, it is important that you take into account the following recommendations:

*Vaccination record with the corresponding immunizations.
*National Anti-rabies Certificate issued by a veterinarian.

*Current health certificate issued by a registered veterinarian.

*Animal health inspection certificate issued by INSAI.

*You must pay the cost of transporting your pet at the time of the check-up. (Consult rates through our Call Center)

*All animals transported in the passenger cabin must weigh up to 6 kg including the container (Kennel).
